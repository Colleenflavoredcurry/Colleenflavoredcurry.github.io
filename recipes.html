Brownies

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 16
Quantity Ingredients
1/2 cup Butter
1/4 teaspoon Baking powder
1 cup White sugar
2 Eggs
1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
1/3 cup Unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 cup Flour
1/4 teaspoon Salt

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.

Step 2: In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.

Step 3: Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.

Step 4: Enjoy.

Cupcakes

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 20-25
Quantity Ingredients
2 cups Flour
1/2 teaspoon Salt
2 teaspoons Baking powder
1/2 cup Butter
1 cup Sugar
2 Eggs
1 cup Milk
2 teaspoons Vanilla essence

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375f or 190c; line muffin cups with papers.

Step 2: Cream butter and sugar till light and fluffy. beat in eggs one at a time.

Step 3: Add flour (mixed with baking powder and salt) alternating with milk beat well; stir in vanilla.

Step 4: Divide evenly among pans and bake for 18 minutes. Let cool in pans. .
